How much does it cost to change your name on a Royal Caribbean Line?

The cost to change your name on a Royal Caribbean Line varies based on timing and ticket type. Typically, minor corrections (like fixing a typo) are free if done early. However, full name changes may incur fees, usually ranging from $50 to $100 or more. Name changes can be made free of charge until 76 days before departure. From 75 days before departure, a fee of $50 per name change will apply.

Can I change passenger name on Royal Caribbean Line?

Name changes are generally allowed before final payment, though fees may apply. After final payment, changes may be restricted or require cancellation and rebooking, potentially incurring penalties.
